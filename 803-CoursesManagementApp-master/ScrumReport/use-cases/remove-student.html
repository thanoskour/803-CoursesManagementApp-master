Remove Student
Data:
  • <Student item>
Actors
  1. Professor
Pre Conditions
  1. Should be viewing a particular course item
  2. Should exist at least one student item
Main Flow:
  1. Professor issues "Delete Student" command
  2. System issues prompts professor with an 'Are you sure?' alert
  3. Professor confirms the deletion of an item
  4. List of students removes the selected item
Post Conditions
  1. List of students is updated without the recently removed student item
