Press Remove Button
Data:
  • <Course item>
Actors
  1. Professor
Pre Conditions
  1. Should exist at least one course item
Main Flow:
  1. Professor issues "Delete" command
  2. System issues prompts professor with an 'Are you sure?' alert
  3. Professor confirms the deletion of an item
  4. List of courses reactively removes the selected item
Post Conditions
  1. List of courses is updated without the recently removed course item
